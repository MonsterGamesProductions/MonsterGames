About City Train Game

Description

Experience highly detailed 3D train models in City Train Game, featuring fully designed passenger coaches and an immersive driver cabin. Step into the role of a train operator and control your train directly from the driver’s compartment, while enjoying stunning scenic views from multiple camera angles. Take charge as a city subway train driver, make quick decisions, ensure passenger safety, and reach each destination on time to prove your skills as a top operator. Challenge yourself in racing mode, where you must beat the clock and manage tight schedules during peak hours. Enjoy a realistic and engaging train simulation experience that brings the excitement of modern rail transport to life.

Operating System Support

The game is compatible with a broad range of devices, providing flexibility for players to enjoy it on their preferred platform .City Train Game is designed to run smoothly across multiple operating systems, ensuring wide accessibility for users:

Android: Android smartphones, Android tablets, Android TV

iOS: iPhone, iPads

Fire OS: Fire OS devices, Fire TV

Windows: Windows PCs and laptops

Input Control Support

City Train Game supports multiple input methods to deliver a comfortable and responsive gaming experience:

Touch controls (for smartphones and tablets)

Keyboard and mouse (for PCs and laptops)

Gamepad controllers

Android TV remote
